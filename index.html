Mi reina.

I know people see many things…

But I see the woman who wakes up and goes to the gym…

who works hard…

who plans her life carefully…

who is disciplined and intelligent…

and still has a smile that makes everything lighter.

I see the side that laughs at random things…

the side that acts like a baby with me sometimes…

the kisses that feel real…

and the little yawn that tells me you want to end a call.

I still remember January…

We were both scared.

You thought distance, age, and everything around us would be too hard.

But we talked.

We cried.

And we stayed.

And now… everything feels stronger.

“You once told me I am your rey.”

I never forgot that.

I know sometimes you worry about getting older…

I know sometimes you think I might leave…

But what I feel for you isn’t something temporary.

I am here because I want to be here.

You bring me peace.

You bring me happiness.

You bring value into my life in a way no one else has.

Until I can hold you for real…

Te amo, mi reina.

I’m still here.
